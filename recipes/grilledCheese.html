Grilled Cheese

Description

Super quick and easy recipe; Two pieces of bread with some cheesey cheese in between, slap in on top of a pan, let the bread grill and the cheese melt, there you go!

The perfect snack / meal when you really just don't want to cook nothin, but you want some sort of sandwich to help fill your belly more than whatever snacks are just sitting there.

Ingredients

  • Pan
  • Spatula
  • 2 slices of bread(whichever your preference)
  • 1-2 slices of cheese(preferably cheddar)
  • 1-2 scoops of butter

Quick and easy cookin' steps

  1. Heat up a pan to be hot enough to melt the butter
  2. While pan is heating up, place some cheese in between your two slices of bread
  3. With pan heated up nice and ready now, drop your scoops of butter into the pan, melting and spreading it around the pans surface
  4. With the butter melted good and spread nice around the pan, place your sandwich flat in there!
  5. Let the one side of the sandwich get nice and crispy brown(helps to use spatula to press down the sandwich into the pan), once its to your liking, flip the sandwich to the other side and repeat
  6. Enjoy that delicious grilled cheesy!
